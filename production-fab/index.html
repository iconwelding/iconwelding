Production Fabrication

Consistent, repeatable parts built to specification at scale.

High-Volume, Repeat-Order Solutions

Icon Welding provides production fabrication services for customers who require consistent, repeatable parts built to specification at scale. We support short-run through high-volume production where precision and repeatability are non-negotiable.

Production fabrication commonly includes brackets, frames, assemblies, sub-components, and welded parts produced in quantity from carbon steel, stainless steel, or aluminum. Each job is approached with the same attention to weld quality and dimensional accuracy as a custom one-off.

We also support customers transitioning from prototype to production, refining designs for manufacturability, strength, and cost efficiency before full-scale fabrication begins. For repeat customers, we maintain part documentation and welding procedures to ensure consistency across orders.
