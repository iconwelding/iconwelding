Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to your most pressing questions about Icon Welding.

What areas do you service?

We are located in Alliance, OH and our main customer base falls within the greater Northeast Ohio area, including Akron, Canton, Massillon, Youngstown, and New Philadelphia. However, for large scale or commercial projects we are willing to travel outside this area as needed.

Do you follow AWS standards?

All welding and fabrication work is performed in accordance with applicable AWS (American Welding Society) standards and industry best practices. Icon Welding follows approved welding procedures, proper joint preparation, and inspection criteria relevant to each project type and material.

What are the rates of your services?

Residential, commercial, and industrial production all have unique quoting processes. Please reach out via phone or email for the most accurate information. Quotes are always free.

What type of warranty do you provide?

Our default warranty is one year from the project completion date. However, not every situation or project is the same and your individual project may require custom terms.

Are you licensed and insured?

Required welder qualifications and certifications are maintained for the scope of work being performed. When a project calls for specific code compliance, documentation, or inspection requirements, those are addressed upfront during the quoting process.

We also maintain industry standard general liability insurance.
