Commercial Kitchen Repairs

Stainless steel sink and work surface repair for commercial kitchens.

Restore Structural Integrity & Sanitation

Icon Welding provides professional commercial kitchen sink repair services designed to restore structural integrity, sanitation, and long-term reliability. Whether you're dealing with cracked welds, leaking seams, or damaged supports, we deliver precise, food-safe repairs.

Commercial sink repairs often require more than a surface fix. We routinely repair stainless steel sinks with issues such as stress cracking, failed seams, damaged legs or supports, and compromised drain flanges or fittings — all performed with food-service-compliant materials and techniques.

We understand that many restaurants or other commercial kitchens have limited flexibility to deviate from their normal hours of operation, so we offer after-hours service calls upon request in order to minimize disruption.

Icon also handles non-standard and problem-solving repairs, including older or discontinued sink models, custom-built sinks, and installations that were improperly fabricated or installed originally.
