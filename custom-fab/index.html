Custom Fabrication Projects

No project is too big or too small. Your imagination is the limit.

Tailored to Your Exact Needs

Icon Welding provides custom welding and metal fabrication services tailored to your exact needs. Whether you bring detailed plans or a rough concept, we work efficiently and precisely to deliver durable, well-executed results.

Custom fabrication projects can range from one-off functional builds to complex assemblies requiring tight tolerances and durable welds. Icon Welding routinely fabricates brackets, handrails, guards, frames, enclosures, art pieces, equipment components, and more across carbon steel, stainless steel, and aluminum.

We also take on specialized and problem-solving fabrication work, including equipment modifications, repairs, replacements for discontinued parts, and custom solutions where off-the-shelf components fall short. If you have a project with unique requirements, we want to hear about it.
