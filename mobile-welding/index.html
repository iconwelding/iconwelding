On-Site & Mobile Welding

Our mobile welding capabilities provide comprehensive solutions for a wide range of locations, worksites, and facilities with unique access or operational requirements.

We Come to You

When equipment or structures can't be brought into a shop, Icon Welding provides reliable mobile welding and on-site repair services to keep your operation moving. We travel to your location with the equipment needed to perform quality welds in the field.

On-site repairs commonly involve cracked welds, broken brackets, damaged frames, worn mounts, structural failures, and fatigue-related issues caused by vibration, impact, or corrosion. We service carbody equipment, agricultural machinery, construction equipment, industrial structures, and more.

We also specialize in field problem-solving where standard repair methods fall short — equipment modifications, reinforcement of high-stress areas, custom-fabricated replacement parts, and emergency repairs to get you back up and running.
