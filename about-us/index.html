About Us

Est. 2023. A full service welding and fabrication company with quality and character at our core.

Who We Are

Icon Welding is a locally owned small business established in Alliance, Ohio in 2023, built with the goal of becoming the region's most reliable resource for professional welding and metal fabrication.

We believe moral character is an essential part of running a successful business. Integrity, honesty, and accountability guide how we quote work, communicate with clients, and execute every project. Our clients can expect straightforward pricing, honest communication, and work that stands behind itself.
