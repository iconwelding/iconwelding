Trailers & Flatbeds

Custom repairs, upgrades, builds, or customizations for trailers and flatbeds.

Restore, Rebuild, Customize

Icon Welding provides trailer and truck flatbed repair and customization services designed to restore strength, improve functionality, and extend service life. Whether the job involves damage repair, structural reinforcement, or a full custom build, we approach each project with the same commitment to quality.

Trailer and truck flatbed projects can range from minor structural repairs to extensive rebuilds and custom configurations. Icon Welding routinely handles frame repairs, crossmember replacement, decking, stake pockets, rub rails, tie-down anchors, gooseneck adapters, and custom toolbox or equipment mounts.

We also take on specialized and problem-solving trailer work, including equipment-specific modifications, custom layouts, and repairs where factory parts are unavailable or inadequate. If your trailer has a unique or non-standard issue, we're equipped to handle it.
