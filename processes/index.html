Welding Processes

We specialize in a comprehensive range of welding processes, including MIG, TIG, Stick, Flux-Cored and more, allowing us to work with carbon steel, stainless steel, aluminum, and beyond.

MIG / GMAW

Gas Metal Arc Welding is a fast, efficient process ideal for fabrication, repairs, and production work. We use MIG welding for clean, strong welds on carbon steel and stainless steel across a wide range of material thicknesses.

TIG / GTAW

Gas Tungsten Arc Welding provides maximum control and precision, making it the preferred process for stainless steel, aluminum, and detailed custom work. We use TIG welding when weld quality, appearance, and tight tolerances are the priority.

Stick / SMAW

Shielded Metal Arc Welding is a versatile, reliable process well suited for structural work, heavy repairs, and outdoor conditions. Icon Welding uses stick welding where accessibility, portability, and penetration on thicker material are required.

Flux-Core / FCAW

Flux-Cored Arc Welding offers deep penetration and high deposition rates, making it ideal for thick materials and structural applications. We use flux core welding when strength, speed, and reliability on heavier material are the priority.

Plastic Welding

Used to repair and fabricate thermoplastics such as tanks, liners, and industrial components. Icon Welding applies proper plastic welding techniques to restore structural integrity and extend service life without replacement.

Brazing

Brazing joins metals using a filler material without melting the base metal, making it ideal for thin materials, dissimilar metals, or where other processes may not be an option. We use brazing where joint integrity and controlled heat input are critical.
